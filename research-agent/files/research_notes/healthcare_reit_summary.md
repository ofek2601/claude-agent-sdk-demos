# Healthcare REIT Financial Performance - Executive Summary

## Research Completion Date
February 28, 2026

## Research Scope
Comprehensive analysis of U.S. healthcare REITs focusing on:
1. Historical and current performance metrics (2020-2026)
2. Correlation between aging demographics and REIT valuations
3. Key healthcare REIT players and property portfolios
4. Occupancy rates, rental growth, and lease spreads

---

## Critical Findings

### 1. Strong Sector Performance - Healthcare REITs Outperforming
- **2024 Return**: 24.2% (3rd best sector)
- **2025 YTD Return**: 14.0% (2nd best sector)
- **2021 Return**: 16.3% (recovery from pandemic)
- **Historical Challenge**: Negative returns in 2022

**Implication**: Healthcare REITs have recovered strongly from COVID-19 and are outpacing broader market expectations.

---

### 2. Demographics Drive Valuations - "Silver Tsunami" Effect

#### Population Aging Metrics
- **2030 Target**: 18 million Americans age 80+ (30% increase)
- **85+ Population Growth by 2035**: 60% increase
- **2034 Milestone**: First time 65+ will outnumber under-18 population
- **Healthcare Spending 85+**: $35,000+ per person annually

#### Market Response
- **Healthcare REIT NAV Premiums**: 33-58% above NAV (June 2024)
- **6 of 10 largest REIT premiums** in healthcare sector
- **Supply Constraint**: Only 1.3% YoY inventory growth
- **Market Implication**: Strong structural demand growth with supply-constrained market

**Correlation Confirmed**: Aging demographics directly correlate with healthcare REIT valuations, evidenced by premium pricing and capital deployment acceleration.

### 4. Occupancy Performance - Strong Operational Execution

#### Overall Occupancy Improvement
- **2022-2024 Trend**: 82.8% → 86.0% (330 basis point improvement)
- **Senior Housing Average**: ~91% occupancy
- **Medical Office Range**: 91-95% across major operators

#### Major REIT Performance (2024)
- **Welltower Medical Office**: 94.4% occupancy (industry-leading)
- **Healthcare Realty Trust**: +150 bps improvement (full year 2024)
- **Ventas Outpatient Medical**: +300 bps improvement
- **Healthpeak Properties**: 91% occupancy, +70 bps absorption

**Operational Excellence**: Occupancy metrics demonstrate robust tenant demand and effective property management.

---

### 5. Rental Growth & Lease Spreads - Strong Pricing Power

#### 2024 Lease Economics
- **Healthcare Realty Trust**: 3.7% cash leasing spreads
- **Healthpeak Outpatient Medical**: 4.7% renewal rent growth
- **Healthpeak Lab Leases**: 6.0% rent growth
- **Healthpeak Overall**: 7.0% renewal rate growth (exceeds historical norms)

#### Leasing Volume
- **Healthcare Realty Trust**: >400,000 sq ft quarterly volumes
- **Healthpeak**: 6.2 million sq ft leased (2024)

**Pricing Power Confirmed**: Strong rent growth (4-7% annually) validates demand strength and limits supply.

---

### 6. NOI Growth - Healthcare Sector Leading Economy

#### Same-Store NOI Growth (2024)
- **American Healthcare REIT**: 17.7% full year (Q4: 21.6%)
- **SHOP Segment**: 52.8% growth (senior housing operating properties)
- **Healthcare Sector Overall**: 7.7% (vs. 2.9% median for all REITS)

#### FFO Performance (2024)
- **Healthpeak Properties**: FFO $1.81/share
- **Ventas**: Normalized FFO $3.15/share, 5% YoY growth
- **Sabra Health Care**: Normalized FFO $1.36-$1.39/share

**Growth Acceleration**: Healthcare REITs demonstrating earnings growth 2.6x broader REIT sector.

---

### 7. NAV & Valuation - Market Pricing Strong Growth

#### Premium to NAV (June 2024)
- **Welltower**: 58.8% premium ($104.25 vs. $65.67 NAV)
- **CareTrust REIT**: 39.4% premium
- **Omega Healthcare**: 39.2% premium
- **National Health Investors**: 33.4% premium

#### Implication
Premium valuations reflect:
- Demographic tailwinds (85+ growing 60% by 2035)
- Supply constraints (1.3% YoY inventory growth)
- Strong rent growth (4-7% annually)
- Capital deployment at accretive yields (10%+)

### 9. Dividend Sustainability - Mixed Signals

#### Red Flag: Welltower
- **Payout Ratio**: 186.03%
- **Issue**: Paying more than earnings
- **Risk**: Dividend sustainability concerns despite strong stock performance

#### Positive Indicators: Other REITs
- Omega Healthcare reduced leverage from 5.0x to <4.0x
- Ventas maintaining strong balance sheet (0.58 debt-to-EBITDA)
- Sector reducing leverage while deploying capital
