# Healthcare REIT Financial Performance Research (2020-2026)

## Executive Summary
The U.S. healthcare REIT sector has demonstrated strong performance since 2020, driven by aging demographics and structural demand tailwinds. The sector achieved a 24.2% total return in 2024 and 14.0% YTD returns in 2025, significantly outpacing broader market performance and general REIT indices.

## Demographic Correlations & Demand Drivers

### Aging U.S. Population Statistics (Key Driver)
- **U.S. population 80+ by 2030**: **18 million** (30% increase from current)
- **Healthcare spending (ages 65-84)**: **$20,000 per person**
- **Healthcare spending (85+)**: **$35,000+ per person**
- **Projected growth (ages 80-84 by 2034)**: **17% increase**
- **Projected growth (85+ by 2034)**: **56% increase**
- **85+ population projected growth by 2035**: **60% increase**

### "Silver Tsunami" Demographic Shift
- **All baby boomers 65+**: Projected by 2030
- **Historic milestone (2034)**: First time 65+ will outnumber under-18 population
- **Current 85+ population growth**: Rapidly accelerating, main demand driver

### Market Supply-Demand Dynamics
- **Year-over-year inventory growth**: Only **1.3%** (significant supply constraint)
- **Demand-supply gap**: Substantial and widening
- **Senior housing acquisitions**: REITs acquiring at prices below replacement cost
- **New development**: Limited supply expansion
- **Demand surge drivers**: Rapidly growing 85+ population
